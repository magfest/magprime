Where do I go when I arrive? How do I pick up my badge?
When you arrive at MAGFest, go to Registration, located in Expo Hall E, to pick up your badge. Registration is open 24 hours a day from Thursday 10am - Sunday 3pm. Follow the green line in this map to help find your way to Registration from any of the three entrances to the hotel. Please bring a government issued photo id such as a driver's license or military id.

Will additional badges be sold at the door?
Yes, but there is a possibility of us selling out, so check our website for any ticket availability alerts. Refer to this FAQ for pricing details.

Which registration line do I use?

  • Staff, Bands, Guests, Vendors, Indie showcase exhibitors, and Panelists share the same line. Look for the signage inside Expo Hall E.
  • All other pre-registered attendees (including those who purchased group registrations) should use the "Pre-Registration Badge Pickup" line inside Expo Hall E.
  • If you are accompanied by people who have not pre-registered, they can purchase one online, or use the self-service registration and payment stations located on the left side of Expo Hall E at the "At-Door Registration" sign. Cash and Credit are accepted. Once registered, proceed to the At-Door badge pickup line to make any needed payments and pick up your badge.

Where can I pick up swag that I pre-ordered, such as a t-shirt or supporter bundle?
After you've picked up your pass, you can pick up your swag at the MAGFest merch booth. The merch booth is located at the front of Expo Hall C. Follow the red line in this map to help find your way to the merch booth from the registration area.

Where can I view a schedule of events?
There are multiple ways to view our schedule:

  • Using the Guidebook app or website (desktop or mobile): Go to the Guidebook website to view the guidebook in your web browser or download the mobile app. Make sure to check for guidebook updates periodically through the weekend, as we may make schedule changes.
  • Paper schedules: Printed schedulse will be available in limited quantities at Registration and our Info Desk. Please consider using guidebook instead to save paper.
