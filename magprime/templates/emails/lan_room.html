LOCATION

GENERAL RULES OF THE LAN ROOM
  • Please follow all LAN staff instructions. This ensures a smooth experience for everyone.
  • Bring up to one monitor, 36 inches or smaller.
  • Bring headphones, not speakers.
  • Bring an ethernet cable 20 feet long or longer. We have a LIMITED STOCK of new ethernet cables for $5 each.
  • Leave networking equipment at home. We have plenty of ports.
  • Bring a power strip. You must only plug your power strip into the one we provide your table. DO NOT DAISY-CHAIN MULTIPLE POWERSTRIPS.
  • Enter and exit only through the checkpoint. Other doors are alarmed.
  • Sleep in your hotel room. Keyboard napping OK; sleeping bags NOT OK.
  • Vape outdoors.
  • Please adhere to the MAGFest Code of Conduct as well as the posted notices in the LAN room as far as conduct and network connections go.


TOURNAMENTS

There are 17 tournaments planned for this year, with some new additions to our classic lineup. Along with our events on the main MAGFest schedule, we have a selection of ad-hoc events running in the background. You can find all of the info on our tournaments on Reddit.

LAN THEATER

This year we will be running two LAN Theaters throughout the event. The Theater entrances are right around the corner from the main LAN room, and is the perfect place to watch many of our premier tournaments. We plan to be livestreaming from the Theater with color commentary, so you can come see the best MAGFest has to offer competing to be #1! We increased the size of the room, so be sure to come by and support competitive PC gaming. Don’t forget to tell your friends to come as well, they might get to the chance to cheer you on!

We also have included some late night content in the LAN Theater, so keep an eye out for that. Stop by and check out what we have planned!
