What Happens Next

After approval, you will be emailed a link to manage your marketplace registration. From this link, you will be able to:
  • Pay for your marketplace registration, which includes the cost of tables and badges.
  • Register your Marketplace Assistants.
  • After your badge and table payment is received, you may add and pay for additional Marketplace Assistants.
Your Marketplace Payment includes your table cost and all badges in your group. After payment, you'll be sent a link by email to follow if you wish to later upgrade your badge. Your Marketplace Assistants will be able to upgrade their own badges and pay for those upgrades on their own.

Later, you will receive another email with more specific information about this year's event.

In the event your Marketplace application is waitlisted, declined, or did not make it through the lottery, you will receive an email. If your application made it through the lottery but is not approved, you will be able to purchase badges for yourself and the number of assistants on the application at the current attendee pre-registration price when the waitlist is exhausted. You will receive an email when these badges are available for purchase.
