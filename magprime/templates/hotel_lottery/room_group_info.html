In an effort to efficiently allocate rooms, anyone planning to room together may form a group of up to four people total. Each individual entry in a {{ c.HOTEL_LOTTERY_GROUP_TERM|lower }} is weighted as though that person entered individually, plus groups of a certain size will qualify for certain room types. This will hopefully reduce cancellation churn.

Creating {{ c.HOTEL_LOTTERY_GROUP_TERM }}s

To form a {{ c.HOTEL_LOTTERY_GROUP_TERM|lower }}:

  1. Choose one person to be the "group leader." They will be the main responsible party, able to update the group's room and/or suite entry, and remove people from the group.
  2. Have the group leader follow the link in their registration confirmation email to complete a lottery entry for a room and/or suite.
  3. After completing a lottery entry, the group leader will receive a confirmation email with a link to create the group and their confirmation number.
  4. After using the link to create a group, the group leader should send the other group members their confirmation number and email address.
  5. The other group members (up to three) can then follow the link in their registration confirmation email to enter the lottery, then use the "Join {{ c.HOTEL_LOTTERY_GROUP_TERM }}" button on their lottery options page. Afterwards, they can enter the group leader's confirmation number and email address to join the group.

When you join a {{ c.HOTEL_LOTTERY_GROUP_TERM|lower }}, your existing entry is automatically withdrawn from the lottery. For example, if you currently have a suite lottery entry, but join a group that only has a room lottery entry, you will only be entered into the room lottery.

Occupancy Limits

{{ c.EVENT_NAME }} policy has set the following occupancy parameters based on hotel policies:
  • A single person in the lottery will not be assigned a double-double room. Double-doubles will be held for roommate groups of 2, 3 or 4 people.
  • A roommate group of 4 people will not be assigned a king room, as king rooms cannot comfortably accommodate 4 people.

Additional Notes on {{ c.HOTEL_LOTTERY_GROUP_TERM }}s

  • {{ c.HOTEL_LOTTERY_GROUP_TERM|capitalize }}s awarded a room are not guaranteed rollaway beds.
  • For privacy and security reasons, {{ c.EVENT_NAME }} is unable to answer questions related to group members or status.
  • By their very nature, hotel bookings include the legal names of all guests. Lottery award notifications and hotel booking confirmations both contain the names of all guests. We strongly encourage people rooming together to know each other well. Should you be uncomfortable with your roommate knowing your identity, or if one of your roommates is uncomfortable with sharing theirs, we gently suggest rooming with others may not be in your best interest.
