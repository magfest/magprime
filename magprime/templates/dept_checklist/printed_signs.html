Room Signage for {{ department.name }}

READ BEFORE SUBMITTING!

Steps:
  1. Open the Signage Inventory spreadsheet. Note: You MUST use your MAGFest login to view the spreadsheet. If you have trouble, please post in #super-signage.
  2. Review the signs we currently have in inventory for your department.
  3. If you see a sign your department no longer needs, please right click the cell with the Primary Text, and add the comment “No longer needed”
  4. Feel free to add additional comments in the Notes cell of each sign.
  5. If you need to request a sign, first use the ctrl+f find feature to see if we already have your requested sign.
  6. If we don't have the requested sign in inventory, please fill out this Signage Request Form.
  7. Continue to use the Signage Request Form for each additional sign you'd like to request.
  8. For any questions, please post in #super-signage.

Signage comes in three types: 12"x18" coroplast, 24"x36" coroplast, and 33"x80" retractable floor banner. All attendee-facing rooms will automatically receive either a 24"x36" coroplast or 33"x80" retractable sign for outside of the room. Please submit requests for additional signage ONLY.

The signage department reserves the right to reduce or decline requests according to their discretion. We will contact you if significant changes to your order are necessary. Please post in #super-signage for any questions, or any odd/complicated requests.

Note: This checklist item is NOT for printed materials such as sign-up forms, waivers, or anything else that goes on standard office paper. STOPS has a paper cutter and a printer for small jobs (less than 50 pages). Anything more than 50 pages needs to go into the Bulk Print Jobs checklist item.
